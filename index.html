Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 2 cans tomato paste (6 oz each)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
  • 12 cooked lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, parsley, salt, sugar, oregano, and thyme to the skillet. Stir well and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  5. Arrange 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  6. Spoon 1/3 of the ricotta cheese over the noodles and spread it evenly.
  7. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  8. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
